Algotime

Algotime is our weekly Leetcode practice session.
Just bring your laptop, your problem-solving spirit, and, of course, your enthusiasm for coding. Whether you're a seasoned coder or just starting out, these sessions are designed to help you grow and learn together.

Competitions

Are you ready to unleash your inner innovator, problem solver, and team player? We invite you to step into the world of competitive programming that will not only challenge your skills but also ignite your passion for excellence.

Hello World Hackathon

If you're new to coding or taking your first steps in computer science, the "Hello World Competition" is the perfect starting point. Join us in this beginner-friendly event, where you can explore the world of programming, showcase your creativity, and bring your first projects to life.

CS Games

For those seeking a more intense challenge, our "CS Games" competition awaits. It's a thrilling battle of wits, where you'll face off against the sharpest minds from universities across the country. It's not just a competition; it's a chance to represent our institution and measure your skills on a broader stage.
